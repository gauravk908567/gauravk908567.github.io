Gaurav Kumar

With a creative and optimistic character and an immense zeal to learn and improve, I bring 2 years of experience working in Unity to the table. My experience includes working on personal projects and serving as a game tester for a leading gaming company, where I was responsible for identifying and reporting bugs, testing game features and providing feedback on the overall player experience. This experience has given me a deep understanding of the game development process, and I have developed a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating engaging and immersive player experiences. My proficiency in Unity's C# and basic knowledge of C++, as well as my experience with Unreal Engine, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator, have allowed me to develop strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills. I am a natural team player who is able to effectively communicate and collaborate with team members. My leadership qualities and ability to think outside the box have also been assets in my projects. I am always open to suggestions and strive to stay current with new technologies to continue to improve my skills.
