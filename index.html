Gaurav Kumar | CAPM | Game Production | Program Manager | Project Manager |

Gaurav Kumar is a dedicated management professional from Varanasi, India, with a lifelong passion for video games and sports. His journey began in childhood, where his love for gaming inspired him to explore the mechanics of game design. By fifth grade, Gaurav was already envisioning ways to enhance gaming experiences. This curiosity led him to delve into game development and assembly programming.

Throughout his college years, Gaurav sharpened his skills by creating 2D and 3D projects, and even published a game on the Google Play Store. His professional career took off at GetMega as a game tester, where he played a pivotal role in ensuring quality for a real-money gaming app. Despite facing health challenges, Gaurav persevered and deepened his expertise in Unity, ultimately joining Gamemano as a Unity game developer.

In a remarkably short time, Gaurav ascended to the roles of development team lead and later scrum master, where he implemented effective agile methodologies to foster collaboration and enhance project delivery. Recently, he earned his CAPM certification from PMI, continually striving to elevate his impact in the gaming industry through leadership, innovation and collaboration.

With a unique blend of creativity and strategic thinking, Gaurav is committed to driving innovation in gaming, making experiences enjoyable for everyone.
